State<'T, 'State>
StateBuilder The state monad. The algorithm is adjusted from my original work off of Brian Beckman's http://channel9.msdn.com/shows/Going+Deep/Brian-Beckman-The-Zen-of-Expressing-State-The-State-Monad/. The approach was adjusted from Matthew Podwysocki's http://codebetter.com/blogs/matthew.podwysocki/archive/2009/12/30/much-ado-about-monads-state-edition.aspx and mirrors his final result.
